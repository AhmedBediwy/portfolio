Systematic Review of Machine Learning Methods for Differential Item Functioning Detection

Mubarak O. Mojoyinola, Ahmed Bediwy, and Juyoung Jung

Abstract

This research reviews machine learning methods for detecting differential item functioning, noting increased publication activity in 2020 and 2023. It categorizes articles by research type, with mixed research being most common, and emphasizes key themes and methodologies, will compare ML methods with traditional methods and offering application recommendations.

Keywords

Differential Item Functioning, DIF, Measurement Invariance, Machine Learning, Supervised Learning, Unsupervised Learning, Deep Learning
