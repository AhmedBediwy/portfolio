Exploring the Efficacy of Including Technology-Enhanced Items in Large Scale Assessments

Ahmed Bediwy, Alexis C. Oakley, Anthony Fina, and Catherine J. Welch

Abstract

In recent years, there has been a rise in conducting large-scale state assessments digitally, creating a demand for new item formats capable of evaluating content knowledge and essential skills more comprehensively. This study aims to assess the performance of TEIs compared to traditional MCIs in large-scale state assessments.

Keywords

Technology Enhanced Items, Multiple Choice Items, Large Scale Assessments, State Assessments
