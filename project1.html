Evaluating the Classification Accuracy of an Adaptive Diagnostic Test

Ahmed Bediwy

Abstract

Adaptive diagnostic tests help teachers identify students' academic strengths and weaknesses. In some cases, the diagnostic results are used to place students into a personalized curriculum. The current study aims to evaluate the classification accuracy of a diagnostic test through simulation to better understand the stability of placement decisions.

Keywords

Computerized Adaptive Testing, Classification Accuracy, Low-Stakes Assessments, Large-Scale Assessment, Cut Scores
